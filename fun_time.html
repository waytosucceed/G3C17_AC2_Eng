Fun Time : Idiom Time


First come, first served: This idiom means that the people who arrive first will be the ones who are served or given an opportunity before others.
Sentence: At the ice cream shop, the policy was first come, first served.

Bird's-eye view: This idiom refers to a view of something from a high elevation as if seen from the perspective of a bird flying overhead.
Sentence: From the top of the tall building, we had a bird's-eye view of the entire city.
